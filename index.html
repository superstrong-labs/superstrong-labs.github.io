Scenarios

Common ways of working together.

A friendly reminder that Superstrong Labs delivers immediate, experienced help without the commitment, costs, and overhead of full-time employees.

Hourly

On-demand consulting is available at $200/hour, rounded up to the nearest quarter hour. Rates are discounted for reserved instances and projects (see below).

Workshops

A great way to initiate working together is to book an on-site workshop. These are useful for quickly reviewing the breadth of existing activities, clarifying your goals, meeting the team, exploring the tools/data/processes in place, and unearthing all those internal debates you've been having. It's also a productive way to get a feel for working together.

Written analysis and recommendations are delivered in advance of or after the workshop as needed.

Reserved Instance (Monthly)

By reserving a monthly block , you’ll get a discounted rate and guaranteed availability. This is for when you consistently need work done, and need someone to be available on chat/phone. You can choose a set schedule or a block of ad hoc hours.

A useful variation is for VC portfolios and incubators to reserve time and allocate it to companies based on need. This removes friction and sprinkles quick wins around.

Reserved instances start at 8 hours/week.

Projects

If you have a discrete project, or a series of sequential projects, we can structure work accordingly. First, we'll dig in to the results you're after, the scope, and the urgency — the usual stuff.

We'll arrive at a flat rate, or at least estimated hours and requirements, along with clear deliverables.
