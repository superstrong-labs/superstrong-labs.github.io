About

Superstrong Labs specializes in marketing consulting, project execution, and special ops.

Clients get hands-on help addressing a range of thorny challenges, like tripling SaaS revenue, scaling an operation to new cities, accelerating e-commerce traction, and producing data that can drive decisions.

Superstrong Labs is led by Robbie Mitchell, a seasoned marketing leader and operator with strong technology chops. Having built his own in-house cross-functional teams, Robbie works easily with business leaders, marketers, salespeople, product owners, designers, and software engineers.

Robbie previously led marketing at Cover (acquired) and Knewton (Series F). You can read more about his experience in this First Round Review profile and on LinkedIn.

Pricing

Common ways of structuring work.

Hourly

On-demand consulting is available, rounded up to the nearest quarter hour. Get immediate help vetting your plans, critiquing your funnel, interviewing a marketing candidate, etc.

Rates are discounted for reserved instances and projects (see below).

Reserved Instance

This is for when you consistently need work done. You'll get a discounted rate and guaranteed availability. This generally involves digging in to your objectives and time frame, then working backward to estimate the level of recurring effort required.

Reserved instances are typically booked in three-month blocks, billed monthly, with work broken out week-to-week. They can, however, be halted at any time with appropriate notice — no hurt feelings.

(Most engagements, regardless of scope, tend to end up being structured as a reserved instance.)

Groups (e.g., VC portfolios, incubators) can also reserve time with a master agreement and delegate it to companies based on need.
