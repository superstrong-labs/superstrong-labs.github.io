Superstrong Labs specializes in marketing consulting services and tools development for startups and technology-centric projects. Featured work includes customer acquisition and engagement, B2B inbound marketing and sales support, automation and reporting, communications, and brand building.

Services

Here is a sample of the types of services offered by Superstrong Labs and its collaborators:

  • Planning: Goals, prioritization, OKRs, KPIs, budgeting
  • Growth cycle: Plan, content strategy, tech development, promotion, measurement, optimization
  • Data: Ontology, tooling, implementation, aggregation, analysis, presentation
  • Development: Website, email, CRM, automation, APIs, custom GUI, custom plugins
  • Customer development: Survey writing and distribution, interviews, customer shadowing
  • Brand: Vision, brand positioning, visual design, copywriting
  • Internal comms: Presentation templates, stationery, business cards, posters, merch

Superstrong Labs can also coordinate the work of others, such as public relations and performance marketing specialists.

Background

Superstrong Labs is run by Robbie Mitchell, a seasoned marketer and leader with wide ranging experience, including leadership at a Series E B2B enterprise juggernaut, leadership at a Series A mobile app, a bootstrapped entrepreneur, and shades in between.

Over time, Robbie developed a reputation for taking on things marketers "don't usually do," such as building and owning an API documentation portal, building a "Mailbox-style" pre-launch site using HubSpot APIs, hacking a live education product into an on-demand product that made millions, commissioning the creation and open-source release of a WordPress deployment framework, and building a company dashboard using SQL, re:dash, and Google Sheets.

You can read more about his experience in this First Round Review profile.
