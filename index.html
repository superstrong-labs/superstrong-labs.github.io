Superstrong Labs specializes in marketing consulting, data cleanup, and tools customization for startups and technology-centric projects. It includes a network of collaborators and partner agencies in design, software development, and data science.

Typical clients include founders doing everything themselves, startups on the brink of making their first official marketing hire, marketing teams looking for stop-gap support, and small teams within bigger organizations.

Superstrong Labs is a full-time endeavor run by Robbie Mitchell, a seasoned marketing leader and operator with wide ranging experience, including a Series E B2B enterprise juggernaut, a Series A mobile app in a two-sided market, a bootstrapped entrepreneur, and shades in between.

Scenarios

Common ways of working together.

Superstrong Labs offers immediate, experienced support with no recruiting delay, long-term commitment, equity, benefits, or office perks.

Hourly

No-commitment, on-demand consulting is available at $200/hour, rounded up to the nearest quarter hour. This is great if you just need an hour or two, or need ongoing but unpredictable help.

Workshops

A great way to initiate working together is to book an on-site workshop. These are useful for quickly reviewing the breadth of existing activities, clarifying your goals, meeting the team, exploring the tools/data/processes in place, and unearthing all those internal debates you've been having. It's also a productive way to get a feel for working together.

Written analysis and recommendations are delivered in advance of or after the workshop as needed.

Workshops are billed at a day rate.

Reserved Instance

You can reserve a monthly block of time. You’ll get a discounted rate and a guaranteed minimum of ad hoc (or scheduled) availability. This is for when you consistently need some work done, and need someone to be available on chat/phone.

A block can also be reserved by a group (e.g., VC portfolio or incubator) and assigned separately. This is useful for giving many companies quick wins while removing the intial contract friction.

Monthly reserved instances start at the equivalent of 1 day/week and scale to full-time.

Projects

If you have a discrete project, or a series of sequential projects, we can structure work accordingly. First, we'll dig in to the results you're after, the scope, and the urgency — the usual stuff. We'll arrive at a flat rate, or at least estimated hours and requirements, along with clear deliverables.
